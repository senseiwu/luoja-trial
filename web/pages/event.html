Name of the game: Super Guess
How to play it
1) Split up different teams and self-introduction in teams
2) One person from each team will come forward and read a word.
3) Then he go back his team and act out the word without speaking.
4) If your team guesses the word put your hand up so shat we can see.
5) The first team to guess the word gets a point, and then someone else comes up to read a word.everyone has chance to act out a word.
6) The team which gets most points wins the game .
